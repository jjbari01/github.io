Project Management
CIS 420: CIS Development Project

An implementation and detailed execution continued from the specifications created in CIS 320: Systems Analysis and Design. Full execution of a website from the specs outlined. Students were grouped into teams.Each member accepted task assignments necessary to meet the need of our customer, KSOA - Kentuckiana Soccor Officials Association.

At the end of the semester, KSOA chose our project out of 7 team sites developed. Our team, The Tomahawk Group was chosen to implement our development. The KSOA website is live and fully functional.
